# Marketing Strategy

## Channels (Priority Ordered)

### 1. LinkedIn Authority (Primary)

**Why:** Direct access to store owners, ecommerce managers, and decision-makers.

**Content pillars:**
- "How Shopify stores lose money without BI" — attention hook
- "Why your Shopify dashboard is lying to you" — pain point
- "The 3 metrics every €1M store ignores" — education
- Case studies (anonymized) — proof
- "What I learned building 10 BI pipelines" — authority

**Frequency:** 3 posts / week
**Target:** Ecommerce managers, DTC founders, operations leads

### 2. Agency Partnerships (High Leverage)

**Why:** Shopify agencies and marketing agencies already have the clients. We become their "data guy."

**Target partners:**
- Shopify Plus Partners
- Digital marketing agencies (especially performance marketing)
- Branding agencies serving DTC brands

**Deal:** 15% commission on first year, or flat referral fee (€500/lead)
**Outreach:** Personalized email to 20 agencies → pitch partnership
**Assets:** One-pager describing the service, case study, partner deck

### 3. Content Marketing (Long-term)

**Distribution:**
- LinkedIn (see above)
- Medium / Substack (long-form)
- Niche communities (Shopify subreddits, ecommerce groups)

**Topics:**
- BI for ecommerce
- Shopify data pitfalls
- Marketing attribution for DTC
- Customer LTV analysis

### 4. US Market Expansion (Medium-term)

**Rationale:** Higher budgets (€3K–€5K setup is small for US), fewer restrictions.

**Approach:**
- Reposition messaging for US audience
- Target mid-market DTC brands ($2M–$20M)
- Same delivery model, USD pricing

## Acquisition Loop

```
Content / Referral → Discovery Call → Demo → Proposal → Onboard
       ↑                                                 │
       └─────────── Case Study ← Results ←──────────────┘
```

**Each client becomes a case study that acquires the next.**

---

## Customer Journey

| Stage | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | LinkedIn post, referral, partner intro | Marketing |
| Interest | Downloads "5 metrics" PDF / free audit | Marketing |
| Discovery | 30-min call, understand pain points | Sales |
| Evaluation | Demo dashboards with their data (sample) | Sales + Delivery |
| Commitment | Proposal, contract, 50% invoice | Sales |
| Onboard | Pipeline setup, dashboard delivery (3–6 wks) | Delivery |
| Retain | Monthly report, quarterly review, upsell | Delivery |
| Refer | Case study, testimonial, referral ask | Marketing |

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Inbound leads / month | 5–10 |
| Discovery → Proposal | 60% |
| Proposal → Closed | 40% |
| CAC (client acquisition cost) | <€500 |
| Net MRR / client | €800 avg |
| Churn / year | <10% |
