# Revenue Model

## Targets

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 |
|---|---|---|
| Active clients | 5–8 | 12–15 |
| Net revenue | €80K–€120K | €150K–€200K |
| Monthly recurring | €4K–€10K | €12K–€20K |
| Average MRR/client | €1K | €1.3K |

## Client Mix Target (Year 1)

| Tier | Clients | Setup Total | MRR Total | Annual |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter (€3K+€500) | 2 | €6K | €1K/mo | €18K |
| Growth (€5K+€1K) | 3 | €15K | €3K/mo | €51K |
| Enterprise (€8K+€2K) | 2 | €16K | €4K/mo | €64K |
| **Total** | **7** | **€37K** | **€8K/mo** | **€133K** |

## Cost Structure

| Cost | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|
| Infrastructure (per client) | ~€50 | ~€600 |
| Cloud tools (Airbyte, dbt) | ~€0 (free tier) | €0 |
| Freelancer / VA | €1K–€2K | €12K–€24K |
| Marketing (ads, tools) | €200 | €2.4K |
| **Total costs** | **~€1.5K** | **~€18K** |
| **Net (at 7 clients)** | **~€6.5K** | **~€85K** |

## Scaling

**Phase 1 (0–5 clients):** Solo delivery
**Phase 2 (5–12 clients):** First freelancer for dashboard setup
**Phase 3 (12–20 clients):** Second freelancer, standardized templates
**Phase 4 (20+):** Productize → self-serve onboarding

## Break-even

At 2 Growth clients (€1K/mo each) + setup fees → ~€18K gross, costs ~€0.5K → **breakeven at 2 clients.**

From client #3 onward, margin is >80%.
