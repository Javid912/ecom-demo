# Pricing Model

## Philosophy

Pricing is not about cost — it's about **value delivered**. A store doing €3M/yr in revenue that gains 10% in marketing efficiency gets €300K/yr in value. Our pricing captures a fraction of that.

## Packages

| | Starter | Growth | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Setup Fee | €3,000 | €5,000 | €8,000 |
| Monthly | €500 | €1,000 | €2,000 |
| Year 1 Total | €9,000 | €17,000 | €32,000 |
| Effective Monthly | €750 | €1,417 | €2,667 |

## Revenue Modeling

### Target: €100K net / yr

**Scenario A — Mix (recommended):**
- 3 Enterprise clients → €96K/yr (no setup)
- Setup fees: ~€10K additional
- **Total: ~€106K** ✅

**Scenario B — Growth heavy:**
- 7 Growth clients → €84K/yr
- 3 Starter clients → €18K/yr
- **Total: ~€102K** ✅

**Scenario C — All Starter:**
- 12 Starter clients → €72K/yr
- Need 2 more clients to hit €100K
- **Total: ~€90K** ❌ (setup fees fill gap)

## Why This Pricing Works

| Reason | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Value capture** | 1–3% of potential efficiency gain |
| **Competitive** | Agency rates in Germany: €150–250/hr |
| **Scalable** | Delivery cost decreases per client |
| **Retention** | Monthly recurring builds predictable revenue |

## Discounting Rules

- **Annual prepay:** 2 months free (16.7% discount)
- **Referral:** 1 month free for referrer and referee
- **Agency partner:** 15% commission on first year
